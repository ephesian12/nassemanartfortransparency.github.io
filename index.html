About Us

NASSEMAN ART FOR TRANSPARENCY (NAFT) is a Non-Profit Non-Government Organization (NPNGO) whose birth was inspired by the vision of Mr. Rabbie Nassrallah, artistically known as NASSEMAN. a Global Anti-Corruption Ambassador for Transparency International FairPlay Music. The purpose of this Organization is to inspire and provide youths opportunities to explore and develop their creative energies geared towards creating awareness through the arts (including music, poetry, drama, film, painting etc.) Additionally, the Organization informs, educates and, through effective communications, provide youths accurate and relevant information on the twin evils of corruption and injustice and the dangers they pose to peace, justice, it national reconciliation and development. The Organization was founded in September 2019 as a Non-Government Organization (NGO). The Organization encourages and promotes social and racial diversity, gender equity, ethnic, harmony, religious, tolerance, and freedom and the association. The Organization hopes to and remains open to forming working and collaborative partnerships and alliances with like-minded institutions and individual locally and globally to further enhance mutual cooperation in the fight against corruptions and injustice both locally and globally.
